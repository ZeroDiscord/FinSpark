# 3-Minute Pitch

Enterprise teams know users are dropping off, but they rarely know which exact feature is responsible, why it is happening, and how to route that insight into execution fast enough to matter.

We built Enterprise Feature Intelligence Platform. It starts by detecting features from an APK or website, generates tracking instrumentation, ingests usage events, processes sessions, predicts churn with our ML layer, and then converts all of that into business-ready analytics and prioritized recommendations.

In our demo, we show a lending platform tenant. We upload the product, detect features like Upload Documents, Credit Check, and Approval Dashboard, and then open the analytics workspace. The dashboard shows adoption, churn concentration, funnel drop-off, and user journeys. In this case, Credit Check is the biggest abandonment point.

But the difference is what happens next. Our recommendation engine turns that pattern into an action card, and with one click it gets pushed into Asana so the product team can act immediately. If the organization runs centralized reporting, the same dataset can be exported into Power BI.

So instead of stitching together discovery, instrumentation, analytics, and product ops manually, we automate the full loop from product behavior to product action.

## 30-Second Version
We built an enterprise product intelligence platform that automatically detects software features, tracks how users interact with them, predicts where customers are likely to drop off, and turns those insights into prioritized actions that can be pushed directly into tools like Asana or Power BI.
